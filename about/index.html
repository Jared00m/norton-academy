Our Story

From our earliest days, we’ve emphasized flexibility, personal growth, and real-world exploration. Each of our students follows a unique educational path, supported by a thoughtful blend of traditional academics, hands-on projects, and interactive learning resources. Today, Norton Academy stands as a testament to the idea that education can be both structured and liberating—preparing students not just for tests, but for life.

  • 3:1 Student-Teacher Ratio: With three students and two dedicated parents guiding the learning process, every child receives highly personalized attention and support.
  • 100% Goal Achievement: Each Norton Academy student consistently meets or surpasses their individualized academic goals, reflecting our commitment to a flexible, effective homeschooling environment.
